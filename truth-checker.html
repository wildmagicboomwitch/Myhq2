📝 Truth Checker
🔐 Just for you — always private
Step 1 of 4
What happened?
Something happened that's making you feel bad. What was it? You can write as much or as little as you want.
Step 2 of 4
What am I telling myself?
Sometimes when something bad happens, our brain tells us really big, scary things. Which of these sounds like what's happening in your head?
Step 3 of 4
What might also be true?
Your feelings are completely real. AND sometimes there are other things that are also true at the same time. What else might be true?
Step 4 of 4
Say something kind to yourself
What would you say to a friend who was feeling this way? Say it to yourself.
